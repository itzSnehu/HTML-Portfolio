The Best Movies According To Sneha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Jesus Film (1979)

It is one of my all-time favorite movies. I grew up watching this film and despite the number of times I watch it, it never bores me. This film is an excellent visual representation of Jesus' life on earth, according to the gospel of Luke.

Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

It is a hindi movie. Both the actor and actress are my favorites. It's a movie, which tells us how to live our life in our own ways by putting our dreams in mind so that we live our dreams, we never get bore from our life.

3 Idiots

Three Idiots' is a remarkable ahead of its time Bollywood blockbuster. This film is a comedy movie with strong, acting, memorable characters, a perplexing storyline and most importantly, highly motivational movie to choose the right path in your life.
